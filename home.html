Explore Indian Heritages by Region

Discover the diverse cultural and architectural treasures from every corner of India.

North India

Home to the Himalayas, the Taj Mahal, and vibrant festivals. Explore spiritual cities and majestic forts.

South India

Famous for temples, beaches, classical dance, and spices. Rich in Dravidian architecture and heritage.

East India

Land of monasteries, festivals, and natural beauty. Explore Odisha, Bengal, and the Northeast hills.

West India

Experience the royal heritage of Rajasthan, vibrant Gujarat, and coastal beauty of Goa and Maharashtra.

Central India

Heartland of India with rich tribal culture, wildlife, and heritage sites like Khajuraho temples.
